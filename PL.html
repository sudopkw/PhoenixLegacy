Phoenix Legacy [PL] is an International, Para-Military like Gang from the Roblox Game "Criminality ". Here, everyone is welcome, even people who have never played Criminality before; but, to become an actual member, it is necessary to attend a Basic Training and to reach level 15 or to buy the "Prime "Gamepass. We also have a Communication Server to organise ourselves to make events such as Deployment and Recruitments. We mainly operate in European Casual Servers. The Leader of the Gang is the General XDavide_09.

The ultimate PL Rank-Up Guide

Q : How do I Rank-up? A: Participate in deployments, Trainings, And other Gang activities to earn RP. Q : What is RP? A: Rank Points, Or RP for short is a Rank-up system we use to help members progress trough ranks rather than ranking them up randomly. The higher your current rank is, the more RP you need in order to rank up, Unless of course, you apply for a rank. Note: Applying for a rank also requires a certain amount of RP Ranks above Non-Commissioned-Officer are apply only. De-Rank information:
Not attending deployments without a valid/approved reason, team killing, not listening to orders, freelancing, etc. will make you loose your RPs. Note for NCOs and above: De-Ranking will be more common than ranking up. We want high activity members who listen to orders , Not people who roam around and do nothing wearing our uniform. You will loose RP at a much faster rate than earning it.
